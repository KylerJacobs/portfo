Introduction

My passion for technology stems from its ubiquitous presence in our lives and the boundless knowledge it holds. I find the desire for continuous learning and growth both humbling and invigorating. In this digital age, where information knows no bounds, technology serves as an ever-expanding realm of opportunities that seamlessly integrates into every aspect of our lives.

Education

I hold a bachelor's degree in Crop Management from Washington State University, graduating in 2018. While my educational foundation may not directly align with technology, it has instilled in me a strong work ethic, attention to detail, and problem-solving abilities.
